What is a 36-Hour Fast?

The 36-hour fast, or "Monk Fast," is an advanced protocol involving not eating for an entire day. For example, you eat dinner Monday, fast through all of Tuesday, and eat your next meal on Wednesday morning. This prolonged fast significantly boosts autophagy (cellular cleanup), enhances insulin sensitivity, and can provide a powerful metabolic reset. It is typically done no more than once a week.
