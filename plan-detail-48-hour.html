What is a 48-Hour Fast?

A 48-hour fast is an extended fast that involves not eating for two full days. For example, after dinner on Monday, you would not eat again until dinner on Wednesday. This highly advanced technique is used to induce a state of deep autophagy, potentially reset the immune system, and profoundly improve metabolic health. This should only be attempted by very experienced fasters under guidance.
