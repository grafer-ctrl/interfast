What is 18:6?

The 18:6 method is a more advanced level of intermittent fasting, extending the fasting period to 18 hours. This shorter 6-hour eating window can enhance the benefits of fasting, such as increased autophagy (cellular cleaning) and improved insulin sensitivity. It's ideal for those who are already comfortable with 16:8 and want to deepen their fasting practice.
