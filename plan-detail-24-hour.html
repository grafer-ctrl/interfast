What is a 24-Hour Fast?

Also known as Eat-Stop-Eat, this method involves fasting for a full 24 hours, once or twice a week on non-consecutive days. For example, you would eat dinner on Monday, then not eat again until dinner on Tuesday. It's a powerful way to reduce calorie intake and trigger deep metabolic benefits without daily fasting commitments.
