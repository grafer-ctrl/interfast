About INTERFAST

What is INTERFAST?

INTERFAST is your personal assistant for navigating the world of intermittent fasting. We provide simple, clear guides for various fasting plans, tools to help you stay on track, and information to support your health journey.

Our goal is to make intermittent fasting accessible and understandable for everyone, from beginners to experienced practitioners.

Our Philosophy

We believe that intermittent fasting is more than just a diet; it's a lifestyle that can lead to profound health benefits, including improved metabolic health, increased energy, and enhanced mental clarity. By focusing on *when* you eat, not just *what* you eat, you can unlock your body's natural ability to heal and thrive.

Important Disclaimer

The information provided in the INTERFAST application is for educational purposes only and is not intended as a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition or before undertaking any new health regimen. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay in seeking it because of something you have read in this application.
