What is 16:8?

The 16:8 method is the most popular intermittent fasting plan. It involves fasting for 16 hours and eating all your meals within an 8-hour window. This balanced approach is perfect for beginners and sustainable for long-term health benefits like improved metabolism and cellular repair.
